# Weekly Notes

Weekly journal entries, reviews, and planning go here.

## Purpose

Capture weekly:
- Weekly reviews
- Progress tracking
- Planning for next week
- Reflections on the past week
- Goal setting

## Template

Use `Template_02. Weekly Note.md` from templates folder.

## Naming Convention

`YYYY-Wxx.md` (e.g., `2025-W03.md` for week 3 of 2025)
