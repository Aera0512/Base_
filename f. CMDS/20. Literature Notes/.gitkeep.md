# Literature Notes

Reading notes and literature reviews from books, papers, articles, and other sources.

## Purpose

Capture insights from:
- Books
- Academic papers
- Articles
- Podcasts
- Videos
- Courses

## Workflow

1. Read/consume content
2. Take notes here
3. Extract key concepts
4. Move refined concepts to `30. Permanent Notes/`
5. Link to relevant CMDS categories
