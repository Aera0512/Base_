# Permanent Notes

Permanent, refined notes that represent your own thinking and understanding.

## Purpose

This is the core of your knowledge system:
- Evergreen notes
- Original insights
- Synthesized understanding
- Refined concepts

## Characteristics

Permanent notes should be:
- **Atomic** - One idea per note
- **Connected** - Linked to related notes
- **Permanent** - Written for your future self
- **Original** - In your own words

## Workflow

1. Start with literature notes or daily notes
2. Extract core ideas
3. Write in your own words
4. Add connections to other notes
5. Store here permanently
